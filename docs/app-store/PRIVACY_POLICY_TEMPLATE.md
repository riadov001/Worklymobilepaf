## 1. Introduction

MyTools Admin ("the Application") is operated by **MyTools Group** ("we," "us," or "our"), a company registered in France. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application.

This application is exclusively reserved for administrators and employees of MyTools Group partner garages ("Authorized Users").

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Account Data
When your account is created by MyTools Group, we store:
- Full name (first name, last name)
- Professional email address
- Encrypted password
- Role (admin or employee)
- Associated garage identifier

### 3.2 Business Data
When you use the application, we process the following **on your behalf**:
- Client information (name, email, phone, address)
- Quotes and invoices (amounts, services, dates, status)
- Appointment reservations (date, time, client, service)
- Photos attached to quotes (stored on our secure servers)

### 3.3 Technical Data
We automatically collect:
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Connection timestamps (for security auditing)
- Error logs (for app stability)

### 3.4 Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect location data
- We do not use advertising identifiers
- We do not track user behavior for marketing purposes
- We do not share data with advertising networks

## 5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:
- Provide the garage management features of the Application
- Authenticate your access securely
- Ensure the security and integrity of the service
- Diagnose technical issues and improve performance
- Comply with legal obligations

---

## 6. Data Storage & Retention

- All data is stored on servers located within the **European Union**
- Account data is retained for the duration of your contractual relationship with MyTools Group, plus 5 years for legal compliance
- Business data (invoices, quotes) is retained for **10 years** in compliance with French accounting law (Code de commerce, Article L123-22)
- Technical logs are retained for **90 days**

---

## 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except:
- **Service providers** acting as data processors under our instructions (hosting, infrastructure)
- **Legal authorities** when required by law or court order

---

## 8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.2+
- Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
- Access tokens expire and rotate automatically
- API access is authenticated via JWT tokens

---

## 9. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a data subject, you have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and associated data (available directly in the app)
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured format
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (where processing is consent-based)

To exercise your rights, contact: **contact@mytoolsgroup.eu**

### Account Deletion
You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the application:
1. Open the app
2. Go to the Dashboard
3. Tap the trash icon in the header
4. Confirm deletion (two-step process)

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible.

## 10. Cookies & Tracking

The Application does not use cookies in the traditional web sense. We use:
- **Secure session tokens** stored in your device's secure storage (not accessible by other apps)
- **No advertising cookies** or cross-app tracking
